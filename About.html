Agency foundation

"Fumo Jisora" is the story of the emergence of a modeling agency, born in the mind of a 19-year-old from Kharkiv. In the early days of solitude, he fearlessly embodied his ideas, creating unique images. Over time, he drew talented models into his world , finding support and inspiration in a team that shared his passion for fashion and beauty.

In my plans:

The most important thing I would like to highlight for myself is to assemble an even more cohesive team, providing myself with greater opportunities in my work. I also want to mention my idea of organizing my first small fashion show in the center of Swiss cities Basel or Zurich. I really hope to accomplish this by the end of 2024.

Project implementation

The project implementation is solely driven by the initiative of the project founder. Additionally, there is no sponsorship, and all finances for the creation come from the founder's personal resources. Due to these reasons, there are some challenges with the speed of establishing and realizing this modeling agency.

team composition

Currently, the team consists of two people. They are also working on just one model, although there are already plans for four. There is nothing wrong with that, as everyone starts somewhere.
